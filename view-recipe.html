BROCCOLI AND BLUE CHEESE QUICHE

Eva used to make this for buﬀets, great also cold

Preparation: 20 minutes
Cooking: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 150g butter
  • 3 1/2 dl plain ﬂour
  • 2 tbsp cold water
  • 1 broccoli, stem cut oﬀ
  • 1 packet of bacon
  • 3 eggs
  • 1.5 dl single cream
  • 1 creme fraiche
  • 150g shredded Roquefort cheese
  • Salt and peppar

Method

Knead together the ingredients for the dough. Put aside to cool for an hour. Massage out until it covers the base and sides of a good sized pie dish, best with detachable sides.

Take the stems oﬀ the broccoli and boil or steam them until they are soft but crunchy. Leave them to cool and dry. Meanwhile, fry the bacon until crispy. Dry them on a sheet of kitchen roll.

Mix cream, creme fraiche, egg, shredded cheese and salt and peppar. Put the broccoli and bacon into the pie shell and pour the egg mixture on top. Cook at 200°C for 20 minutes.

Comments

Can be eaten also cold! Be careful not to overdo the sauce, or the quiche will be runny.
